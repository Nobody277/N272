Course Description

This course provides an introduction to the basic principles of cell biology. Topics covered include cell structure, cellular organelles, membrane transport, cellular metabolism, cell division, and cellular communication. The course includes both lecture and laboratory components.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the basic structure and function of cells and cellular organelles
  • Explain the processes of membrane transport and cellular metabolism
  • Describe the stages of cell division and cellular reproduction
  • Apply microscopy techniques to observe cellular structures
  • Analyze experimental data and draw conclusions based on evidence
